Section 1: Overview

Originator:Minnesota Department of Natural Resources (MNDNR)

Title: State Trails of Minnesota

Abstract: This dataset represents State Trails that have been legislatively authorized and physically constructed and that are maintained by the MNDNR Division of Parks and Trails.
These trails, which pass through a combination of state-owned lands and acquired easements, have multiple use status with specific activities supported in designated sections.

Purpose: To support trail maintenance, recreation planning, and public access information, with emphasis on cartographic map rendering.

Time Period of Content Date: 01/27/2021

Currentness Reference: These data were collected from GPS, aerial photograph interpretation, and paper maps.

Section 2: Data Quality

Attribute Accuracy: The attributes in this table are undergoing accuracy assessment on an as-needed basis.

Logical Consistency: Data are topologically correct using internal editing tools at the DNR.

Completeness: This dataset includes all state trails that have been legislatively authorized and physically constructed. Proposed alignments and trail segments not yet built are not included. Complete over the area of the State of Minnesota

Horizontal Positional Accuracy: Not specifically assessed
.
